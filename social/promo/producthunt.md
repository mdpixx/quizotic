# Quizotic Product Hunt Launch Materials

## 1. Product Hunt Tagline

Kahoot meets Mentimeter for India

## 2. Short Description

Run live quizzes, polls, word clouds, Q&A and AI-generated decks in one browser-based platform. Built for Indian teachers, coaching institutes and trainers with UPI billing, learning-science reports and a free 50-participant plan.

## 3. Full Description

Quizotic is a free live quiz and interactive presentation platform built for India.

It combines the energy of a Kahoot-style real-time quiz with the flexibility of a Mentimeter-style presentation deck. Teachers, coaching institutes, colleges and corporate trainers can run quizzes, polls, word clouds, Q&A, rating slides, ranking activities and learning checks from one browser-based tool. Participants join from their phones. No app install. No complicated setup. Just create a session, share the code, and run the room live.

The idea is simple: every class, workshop and training session should be more active than a one-way lecture.

### What You Can Build

Quizotic supports 19 slide types and 11 question types, so a session can be more than a quiz. You can start with a warm-up poll, teach with content slides, check understanding with MCQs, collect questions anonymously, build a word cloud, run a leaderboard round, and end with a quick confidence check.

The AI quiz generator helps you move faster. Give Quizotic a topic or learning material and it can draft questions that you can edit, reorder and run live. This is especially useful for teachers and trainers who need to prepare a revision round quickly before class, or for facilitators who want to turn a PDF, lesson plan or workshop outline into an interactive session.

The free plan supports up to 50 participants, which is enough for a typical classroom, coaching batch, college seminar or internal training room.

### Built on Learning Science

Quizotic is not just a game layer. It is built around how people actually learn.

Bloom's Taxonomy helps hosts see whether their quiz is testing recall, understanding, application or deeper thinking. A class may score well on memory questions but struggle when the same concept is applied to a new situation. That distinction matters when you are planning the next lesson.

The Confidence Grid adds another signal: how sure learners are about their answers. A correct answer with low confidence needs different follow-up than a wrong answer with high confidence. Trainers can spot overconfidence, uncertainty and genuine mastery instead of treating every score as the same.

Spaced Retrieval helps turn one-time quiz energy into retention. Concepts that learners miss can come back later instead of disappearing after a single session. That makes Quizotic useful not only for engagement, but for revision, assessment and long-term learning.

### For Teachers

Teachers can use Quizotic for chapter revision, formative assessment, exam preparation, concept checks and classroom games. A 10-minute live quiz at the end of a lesson gives instant feedback: which topic landed, which misconception is common, and which students need help.

Because participants join through the browser, it works naturally in classrooms where students use mixed devices. The free 50-participant plan is designed for real classroom sizes, not just demos.

### For Coaching Institutes

Coaching institutes can run batch-wise quizzes, quick mock rounds, topic drills and leaderboard sessions for JEE, NEET, UPSC, school exams or internal practice. Quizotic keeps the high-energy competition students like, while adding reporting that helps faculty identify weak topics and confidence gaps.

The India-first billing also matters here. Payments through UPI and INR-friendly flows are easier for Indian tutors, small institutes and training businesses than dollar-priced tools that need international cards or awkward expense handling.

### For Corporate Trainers

Corporate L&D teams can use Quizotic for onboarding, compliance checks, induction programs, leadership workshops, safety sessions, product training and town halls. Trainers can mix slides with live polls, anonymous questions, quick knowledge checks and final reports.

For facilitators, this means fewer tools. You do not need one platform for quizzes, another for word clouds, another for Q&A, and another for participant feedback. A single Quizotic deck can carry the full session.

### Why It Is Different

Most global quiz and presentation tools are excellent products, but they were not built around Indian classroom and training realities.

Quizotic is India-first. It is free to start, supports up to 50 participants on the free plan, includes UPI billing, and is designed for teachers, trainers and coaching businesses that want a professional tool without foreign-currency friction.

It also combines two categories that are usually separate. Kahoot-style products are strong for competitive quizzes but weaker for interactive presentations. Mentimeter-style products are strong for audience interaction but often lighter on quiz mechanics. Quizotic brings both together: real-time quizzes, leaderboards and question types on one side; polls, word clouds, Q&A and presentation flow on the other.

The learning-science layer is the third difference. Bloom's Taxonomy, Confidence Grid and Spaced Retrieval make the product more than a fun classroom moment. They help hosts understand whether learning is actually happening.

### Tech Stack

Quizotic is built with Next.js and deployed on Railway. The product uses a real-time quiz engine for live sessions, a browser-based participant experience, AI-assisted quiz generation, and UPI-ready billing for the Indian market.

The stack is intentionally practical: fast to ship, easy to improve, and built for live classroom and training use cases where reliability matters.
