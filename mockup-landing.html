How it works

From idea to live quiz
in under 3 minutes.

No training. No setup. Just open, create, and host — your audience joins from any device.

01
🧠
Create or generate

Build manually or paste a topic, URL, or PDF. Our AI writes your quiz in seconds — questions, options, timers, and Bloom's taxonomy tags included.

02

Hit "Start session". A 6-digit code appears. Participants open quizotic.live/join on their phone — no app install, no account needed.
