## What is Kahoot Page

### How It Works
1. **Create** — build kahoots from topics, PDFs, YouTube, slides, or AI
2. **Play** — host shows questions on a big screen; players join via PIN on personal devices
3. **Share** — distribute kahoots; institutional plans allow co-creation

### Player Flow
- Players join at kahoot.it or app using 6-digit PIN
- No app required — browser works
- Choose a nickname on join
- See questions on shared (host) screen; tap answers on own device
- Asynchronous (self-paced) mode also available
